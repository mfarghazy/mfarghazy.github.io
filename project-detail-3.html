Revo Hospital: Operational Efficiency Analysis

Project Details

Duration: 14 Days
Team: Individual
Tools: Tableau

*Disclaimer: The project is a part of Full-Stack Data Analytics assignment

Overview

Business Background

Revo Hospital faced severe operational bottlenecks and bed capacity blockages. A lack of clear tracking for bed efficiency led to the rejection of new patient referrals, substantial revenue leakage, high staff burnout, and lost market share. Thus, we need to dentify medical departments exceeding Average Length of Stay (ALOS) benchmarks to resolve capacity constraints, secure smooth patient flow, and optimize Average Revenue per Bed Day.

Method

  • Analyzed operational data to identify bottlenecks and track bed utilization across departments and room tiers.
  • Recommended a dynamic bed allocation policy to handle sudden admission surges.
  • Proposed targeted operational audits targeting Deluxe room discharge workflows and the utilization of historical data trends to implement predictive staffing.

Result

  • Isolated Key Bottlenecks: Successfully pinpointed Oncology and Cardiology as the departments locking bed capacity the longest.
  • Uncovered Inefficiencies: Identified a low-margin, long-stay (>4.5 days) anomaly concentrated heavily in Deluxe rooms.
  • Business Impact: Enabled data-driven strategic interventions to eliminate referral rejections, recapture lost revenue potential, and streamline patient turnover.
