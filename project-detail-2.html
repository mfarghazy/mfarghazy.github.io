TheLook E-Commerce: Inventory Growth and Monthly Retention Analysis

Project Details

Duration: 14 days
Team: Individual
Tools: SQL, Spreadsheet

*Disclaimer: The project is a part of Full-Stack Data Analytics assignment

Overview

Business Background

TheLook E-Commerce needed to drive revenue growth, maintain operational excellence, and sharpen its product strategy by uncovering hidden vulnerabilities in its inventory management and customer lifecycle behaviors. Thus, we need evaluating monthly inventory growth trends across top product categories and measuring customer loyalty through a comprehensive monthly cohort retention analysis for all users acquired throughout 2022.

Method

  • Developed and executed optimized queries to track month-over-month inventory changes and calculate user retention timelines.
  • Synthesized structured query outputs into an actionable OBIPR (Observation, Business Impact, Isolation, Prioritization, Recommendation) framework.

Result

  • Uncovered a critical >50% inventory crash in April 2023 (led by Jumpsuits & Rompers at -78.69%)
  • Identified severe user attrition with 2022 customer retention consistently below 3%.
  • Delivered strategic recommendations to mitigate stockout risks via procurement audits and pivoted marketing focus toward post-purchase CRM strategies and tactical clearance sales to fix customer retention.
