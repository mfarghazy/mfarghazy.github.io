About Me

Most people look at data and just see endless rows of numbers. I look at it and see a blueprint.

With a background in architecture, my brain is wired to build things systematically. I treat business challenges like structural design: first, I understand the foundation (your business goals), then I map out the framework (connecting the right data), and finally, I build the solution (clear strategies).

Today, instead of designing physical spaces, I design data-driven solutions. Using tools like SQL, Python, and Tableau, I help businesses spot hidden problems—like missing revenue or stock issues—and turn raw data into decisions that make sense. I don't just crunch numbers; I build the strategy that helps businesses grow with confidence.
