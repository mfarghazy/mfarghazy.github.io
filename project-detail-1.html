IndiRevo E-Commerce: Fulfillment Performance and Revenue Leakage Analysis

Project Details

Duration: 1.5 months
Team: Individual
Tools: Python, Tableau

*Disclaimer: The project is a part of Data End-to-End Portfolio Project (DEEPP)

Overview

Business Background

IndiRevo suffered a 15.71% financial loss—totaling 13.14M INR out of 83.69M INR in gross revenue—due to operational inefficiencies across B2B bulk and B2C retail segments. Thus, we need to identify root causes of delivery failures and recommend data-driven order routing adjustments to reduce logistical revenue leakage by 10% within two months.

Method

  • Owned end-to-end data pipeline: cleaned data, handled outliers, and consolidated complex order statuses.
  • Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA), segment performance analysis, and statistical correlation/impact modeling.
  • Built interactive dashboards to track fulfillment metrics.

Result

  • Discovered B2C retail drove 99.56% of delivery failure risk (13.08M INR), while B2B posed high-value transaction risks (708.92 INR loss/transaction).
  • Proved statistically that Platform-Fulfilled routing cuts B2C delivery failure risk in half (0.50x odds ratio).
  • Recommended automated routing to Platform-Fulfillment for low-performing B2C merchants and strict SLA re-evaluations for B2B 3PL partners.
