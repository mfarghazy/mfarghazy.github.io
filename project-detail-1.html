IndiRevo: Fulfillment Performance and Revenue Leakage Analysis

Project Details

Duration: 10 days
Team: Individual
Tools: SQL, Tableau

*Disclaimer: The project is a part of RevoU Data Insight Project, a 4-week case study designed to analyze customized datasets and address business challenges...

Project Overview

Background

[Finance Industry Application] RevoFin conducted a loan portfolio review to assess portfolio health using TKB30 as a key risk indicator. The analysis aimed to identify high-risk cohorts and understand borrower characteristics.

Method

  • Performed cohort-based analysis by segmenting loans into monthly vintages and calculating OS, ENR, and TKB30.
  • An in-depth profiling was conducted on the lowest-performing cohort, examining borrower income, home ownership, and loan purpose.

Result

  • Anomaly Detection: April 2014 recorded the lowest TKB30 (~93%), driven by a higher share of loans overdue >30 days.
  • Borrower Profile: 60% of borrowers were still paying mortgages, increasing debt burden and delinquency risk.
  • Recommendations: Strengthen underwriting for debt-related loans and enhance monitoring of highly leveraged borrowers.
