RevoBank Customer Segmentation Analysis

Project Details

Duration: 21 Days
Team: Individual
Tools: Python, Spreadsheet

*Disclaimer: The project is a part of Data End-to-End Portfolio Project (DEEPP)

Overview

Business Background

RevoBank needed to transition from traditional portfolio management to a data-driven model to maximize its 1.5% Merchant Discount Rate (MDR) profit margins using 6 months of historical raw credit card and user data. Thus, we need to evaluate sales performance, identify transaction frequency drivers, and execute customer segmentation to provide the product management team with actionable net-profitability strategies.

Method

  • Conducted end-to-end data preparation: missing values, duplicates, account filtering.
  • Performed EDA on transaction behavior and Debt-to-Income (DTI) metrics.
  • Customer segmentation via K-Means clustering utilizing RobustScaler, validating optimal clusters with the Elbow and Silhouette methods.

Result

  • Delivered a net profit of Rp 5.83 B with a 0.2% fraud rate. Identified 4 strategic customer personas: High-Value Elites (primary revenue driver at Rp 15.55 M profit/capita), Steady Growth Transactors, Mass Market (largest segment at 54.2%), and a Risk Group (-Rp 595 K net profit/user).
  • Formulated targeted product strategies, including premium loyalty programs, micro-transaction campaigns, and credit freezes to mitigate defaults.
